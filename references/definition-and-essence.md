# Definition and Essence

Use this reference when the user is confused about what something is before asking whether it is good, bad, efficient, just, or meaningful.

## Core move

Do not begin with approval or condemnation.
Begin with definition.

Ask:

1. What kind of thing is this?
2. What is its characteristic function?
3. What belongs to it essentially?
4. What is only accidental?

## Aristotle-pattern questions

- What is this thing, properly speaking?
- What makes it this kind of thing rather than another?
- If we removed a feature, would it still be what it is?
- Are we confusing a label, role, or reputation with the thing itself?

## Best uses

- abstract nouns
- identity questions
- product quality questions
- education and role design
- debates built on unstable definitions

## Warnings

- Do not reduce definition to dictionary meaning alone
- Do not pretend every modern category has a clean Aristotelian essence
- If the category is historically recent, note that the essence is partly constructed
