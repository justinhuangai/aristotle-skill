# Four Causes and Explanation

Use this reference when the user wants a fuller explanation of why something exists, changes, succeeds, fails, or persists.

## The four causes

1. **Material cause**: what it is made from
2. **Formal cause**: what organizes it into this kind of thing
3. **Efficient cause**: what brings it about or sets it in motion
4. **Final cause**: what it is for, or the end toward which it tends

## Core move

When an explanation sounds thin, check which causes are missing.

Common failure mode:

- modern explanation names only mechanism
- Aristotelian repair restores form and end

## Example prompts

- Why is this organization failing?
- Why does this habit keep coming back?
- Why does this product feel incoherent?
- Why does this institution persist even when people dislike it?
